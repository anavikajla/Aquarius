Company Profile


Aquarius has created a reputed space among headhunting professionals focussing on executive search for top and middle management positions for all functionalities and sectors. Our present clients span sectors covering automotive, speciality chemicals, FMCG, industrial, food, engineering services, textiles, R&D, health, pharmaceuticals, insurance, audit firms & BPO sector.

Unique structure and culture

Aquarius has developed a unique business structure and culture that empowers its consultants to give outstanding service to our clients. Our culture is entrepreneurial, sensitive to the market and is able to react quickly to our clients' dynamic needs.

Our greatest asset: our people

We believe that each of our consultants require a sense of dignity, pride and satisfaction in what they do. Meeting client expectations depends on the united efforts of our consultants, treating candidates with respect and working in unison with our clients.

Deep knowledge of our clients' business

We strongly believe that a first class executive search company must have deep knowledge of a client's business, market and culture in order to find and recommend the perfect candidate. Each assignment is therefore the personal responsibility of our consultants who oversees the search from brief to completion, keeping a watchful eye at every step.

Core Values


Integrity

In the true spririt of openness and transparency, we honestly represent our clients to candidates and our candidates to clients. We deliver what we promise.

Partnership

We operate as an extension of our client's management, working closely to provide decisive and dedicated support. We strive for thorough understanding of client's priorities and deliver accordingly.

Confidentiality

We accord the highest respect to the information we have been entrusted with.

Vision & Mission


Vision

At Aquarius, our vision is to be the premium Indian head hunting and recruitment company, most admired for its people, partnerships and performance. Being premium does not mean being biggest but it does mean being the best in terms of client value, client service, employee talent, and one that enjoys consistent and predictable growth.

Mission

Our performance is unparalleled because we do what we say we're going to do—efficiently and on time. Every client and every assignment gets our undivided attention and focus, and we are committed to delivering total satisfaction by identifying the right candidates that meet the requirements of the job. We deeply value the time of our clients and attempt to complete our assignments as fast as we can. By placing an emphasis on integrity and appropriateness, we conduct reasonable checks on the candidates before recommending their profiles to our clients and support the selected candidates in settling down through valuable mentorship.
